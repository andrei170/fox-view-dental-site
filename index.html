Meet the Doctor

The dentist other dentists learn from.

Dr. Yenchesky has spent 17 years on the faculty of The Clinical Mastery Series, one of the country's most respected post-graduate dental programs. He teaches Ultimate Occlusion, Anterior Aesthetics, and the Art of Photography to practicing dentists across the country. When he sees you, that same standard of care is what you receive.

He earned his DDS from the University of Minnesota in 2002, following a competitive international residency at Aarhus University in Denmark in 2001. From 2010 to 2013 he served as adjunct faculty at the Arizona School of Dentistry and Oral Health. He completes roughly 100 continuing education hours per year, approximately six times the Wisconsin biennial minimum.

Outside the chair, Dr. Yenchesky is known for a down-to-earth demeanor that puts patients at ease, a relentless pursuit of craft, and a family that keeps him rooted in the De Pere community.
